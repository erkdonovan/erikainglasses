Web developer with a love for videos

While growing up in a small farm town, I developed a love for making videos, playing video games, and an affinity for writing. In highschool the opportunity to expand on these skills came with joining the school newspaper, yearbook, and the broadcasting team.

After college I took the jump and started making videos for YouTube. Although the channel hasn’t taken off it allows me to indulge in my love of making and editing videos.

Recently I took another jump and started streaming xbox on Twitch, enjoying sharing my process through Lego games. When not making videos or playing games, I’m writing articles on Medium about web development and life.
